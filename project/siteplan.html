Site Plan: The Long and Winding Web

Site Name

The Long and Winding Web

The Long and Winding Web" is a digital haven for Beatles fans worldwide. Inspired by the timeless song "The Long and Winding Road, this site is dedicated to exploring the legacy of the Fab Four.

Purpose of the Site

The website will act as a resource for fans, featuring content such as biographies, a complete discography, historical trivia, and curated multimedia. It will also include fan-driven sections like a forum and interactive polls.

Color Scheme

The chosen colors reflect a timeless, modern aesthetic while keeping the focus on readability and elegance:

  • Primary Background: #f5f2ea (soft beige for a neutral base).
  • Text and Headers: #1f313e (deep navy for contrast and sophistication).
  • Accent Color: #dc384e (vibrant red for buttons, links, and highlights).

Typography

The selected fonts balance modernity and readability:

  • Header Font: "Exo", used for titles and section headers to provide a bold and futuristic feel.
  • Body Font: "Noto Sans", used for paragraphs and general text for clarity and comfort.

Wireframe

The homepage design will follow a centered and responsive layout, adapting seamlessly between desktop and mobile views:

  • Mobile View: A single-column layout with a compact header, navigation menu, and stacked content blocks.
  • Desktop View: A centered, multi-column layout with a hero section, featured content, and sidebar widgets.
